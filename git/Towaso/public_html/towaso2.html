3 SIMPLE WAYS TO SAVE WATER

Posted by Nikhil Reddy.

Water crisis is something which is certain if we ignore to save water. With small change in our behavior we can save water in day to day life.


1. Filling flush tank with waste water from washing basin. Twice use of same water can save water.


2. Brushing teeth using mug of water can save water up to 4 gallons.


3. Take Bath/ Wash Car using bucket & mug, and save water.
